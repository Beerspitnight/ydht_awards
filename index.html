YOU DON’T HAVE TO AWARD FOR INDEPENDENT AUDIO EXCELLENCE

The award exists to spotlight indie shows that blend originality, research, and just the right amount of chaos—without the benefit of a marketing budget, studio backing, or that weird ad where Joe Rogan sells mushroom coffee.

Each You Don’t Have To Award recipient is chosen with the precision of a late-night scroll through Apple Podcasts and a gut feeling that says, “Yeah, this one’s got it.” There are no entry fees, no velvet ropes, and definitely no influencer judges. Just solid storytelling, a digital badge, and the raw satisfaction of being noticed by someone equally messy in spirit.

The Messy Minded Podcast
Inaugural Winner: The Messy Minded Podcast
YOU DON’T HAVE TO AWARD FOR INDEPENDENT AUDIO EXCELLENCE

Accolades: A fantastically riveting and engaging podcast for curious people with slightly chaotic brains. Host Jess dives into the weird true stories that derailed her focus for the week—covering everything from internet scams and historical con artists to secret societies, Viking adventurers, and liars with remarkable confidence. It’s like a college lecture from your funniest, least linear friend.

About the Award: The You Don’t Have To Award was launched in 2025 by Bruno, host of Bruno’s You Don’t Have To, to celebrate indie podcasters doing deeply human, delightfully unhinged work. No gatekeeping, just good audio.
